The Workout

This workout is modeled after the Easy Strength routine by Dan John. The principle of this routine is to push yourself enough to get stronger, but not so much that you're sore the next day. With consistent lifts at reasonable weights, strength will come easy.

  • Work out consistently (5 days a week)
  • Do the same lifts each day
  • Do only the amount you feel is right. If 4 reps makes sense one day, do it. If 8 makes sense another day, do it.
