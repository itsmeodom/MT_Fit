The Workout

This workout is modeled after the Easy Strength routine by Dan John. The principle of this routine is to push yourself enough to get stronger, but not so much that you're sore the next day. With consistent lifts at reasonable weights, strength will come easy.

  • Work out consistently (5 days a week)
  • Do the same lifts each day
  • Do only the amount you feel is right. If 4 reps makes sense one day, do it. If 8 makes sense another day, do it.
  • Do not push yourself to the point of grunting. (Typically 2 sets of 5)
  • Hit the essential movements

Essential Movements

Upper Body Push - Shoulder Press

Green band, aim for two sets of 5. Sit on a chair.

Upper Body Push - Tricep Extension

Blue band, aim for two sets of 5. Sit on the band, on a chair.

Upper Body Pull - Bent Over Row

Green band, aim for two sets of 5. Step into loop, hands close, and pull band to chest

Upper Body Pull - Bicep Hammer Curl

Green band, aim for two sets of 5

Lower Body - Waffles Front Squat

One corgi, aim for two sets of 5.
Curl band to your chin and perform squat

Hinge Motion - Deadlift

Green band or the two mini red bands, aim for one set of 5

Hinge Motion - Straight Leg Deadlift

Green band or the two mini red bands, aim for one set of 5

Hinge Motion - Sumo Deadlift

Green band or the two mini red bands, aim for one set of 5

Core Strength - Palloff Press

Red Band, aim for 2 sets of 15. One set on each side
